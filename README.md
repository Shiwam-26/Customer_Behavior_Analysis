# 🛒 Customer Behavior & Shopping Analysis

## Project Overview
Understanding how customers shop — what they buy, when they buy, and how much they spend — is critical for any retail or e-commerce business. This end-to-end project analyzes customer shopping behavior using **Python, SQL, and Power BI** to uncover patterns that drive business decisions.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
**Question:** What are the key patterns in customer shopping behavior, and which customer segments contribute most to revenue?

Businesses need to understand purchasing trends, high-value customers, and product preferences to optimize marketing and inventory strategies.

## 📌 Steps Followed
1. **Data Generation & Loading** — prepared and loaded customer shopping dataset using Python
2. **Database Connection** — connected Python with SQL database for querying
3. **SQL Analysis** — wrote queries to extract key business insights
4. **Data Cleaning** — handled missing values and formatted data for analysis
5. **Power BI Dashboard** — built an interactive dashboard for visual storytelling

---

## 📊 Key Insights from SQL Analysis
- Identified **top spending customer segments** by age group and gender
- Analysed **most purchased product categories** and seasonal trends
- Calculated **average order value** across different customer demographics
- Found **high-frequency shoppers** vs one-time buyers
- Tracked **preferred payment methods** and their correlation with spend

---

## 📈 Power BI Dashboard Highlights
- Customer segmentation by demographics
- Revenue breakdown by category and region
- Shopping frequency and purchase trends over time
- Top products and customer lifetime value overview

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations
- Target high-frequency shoppers with loyalty rewards programs
- Focus marketing spend on top-performing product categories
- Create personalized offers for age groups with highest average order value
- Re-engage one-time buyers with targeted discount campaigns
